\begin{abstract}
COVID 19 has had many effects on society, one being the rate of
marriage. In this paper we plot and outline the data of marriage
licenses acquired in Toronto. This will demonstrate how marriage license
quantity declined during COVID, and rebounded hard right after.
\end{abstract}
